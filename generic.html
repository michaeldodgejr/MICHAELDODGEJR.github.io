About Me

I began my journey into Data in 2018. After having built my own computer and graduating High School in 2016 I worked effortlessly to achieve my dream and successfully became a professional Esports Athlete within 2 years. After analyzing gameplay and data daily I discovered the insights that data can give. Blessed with this opportunity I realized my passion for data while developing my leadership skills as Team Captain. Currently, it is my goal to utilize my experience and skills in data wrangling, manipulation, and visualization to help bring data-driven insights to stakeholders.

In my everyday life, I am passionate about improving myself, tech, and helping others in Esports. I'm always keeping an open mind and looking to learn new things. If you're looking for a team member who is dedicated, analytical, and always striving for improvement, please feel encouraged to reach out to me through LinkedIn or email!
