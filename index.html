Netflix Movies & TV Shows Data Analysis

Developed a dynamic and interactive Netflix dashboard using Tableau, showcasing key insights about the platform’s content library. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the top 10 genres and most popular ratings, as well as a comparison between the distribution of movies and TV shows. Additionally, I created a geographic map that displays the distribution of titles by county, giving users a unique perspective on the platform’s reach. This user-friendly dashboard allows users to easily access details about thousands of titles, providing a deeper understanding of Netflix’s content offerings.
